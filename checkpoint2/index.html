The Heart of New Hampshire

Central New Hampshire Kidney Center is a dialysis unit serving the Lakes Region and Central New Hampshire since 1990. Being the only independent dialysis center in New Hampshire, we are able to maintain the highest quality of care for our patients in a family friendly atmosphere. Our center prides itself on having the highest safety records, as we have a highly experienced staff, and our Nephrologist is always on site. The Lakes Region area, and Lake Wiinipesaukee, are the most beautiful of resort areas, and a destination for many dialysis patients during the summer and fall seasons. CNHKC provides care to visiting patients during their vacation.
Anytime of the year is a good time to visit New Hampshire

New Hampshire experiences each of the seasnons to the fullest. The winters are full of snow and all New Hampshirse lakes freeze over. The state has become a winter destination for many who enjoy skiing, ice fishing, snow mobiling, snow showing, and a number of other winter activities New Hampshirse has become known for.

Perhaps the season New Hampshirse is most famous for is Autumn. People come from far and wide to see the New Hampshire foliage which can only be described as awe inspiring.

Spring time brings new life to the New Hampshire area. The states natural landscape and rural settings give New Hampshire springs a sense of real rejuvination.

No matter what the season, New Hampshirse has so much to offer. The summers are warm with beautiful scenary and there are many outdoor adventures to be had.
